Boda Banja

In September, 2016 BodaBoda Banja was incorporated. They finance Bajaj two and three-wheeler variants under a hire purchase scheme to customers. They are unlocking growth in Uganda by empowering Boda Boda riders to own their business - often considered too risky for credit.

Riders have a new bike in less than 3 days. They own the asset after successfully completing the weekly installments. The objective of the company is to have as many Boda Boda riders own the assets over a period of time instead of taking the vehicles on rent - as is the case with other providers.

The brief was to create a system to be used internally by stuff to register employees within the company, asssign roles (administrator, supervisor or sales executive - each with unique permissions), work stations and keep track of their activities in terms of how many customers they register. The registered employees then login with a username and password generated by the system.

Through interactions with the client at the startof the project, I understood their mission and position in the marketplace. I also understood the client's organisation culture and what they expected of their employees. These interations were eventually important in providing guidelines for both the user experience and interface designs.
